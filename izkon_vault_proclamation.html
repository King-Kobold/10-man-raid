A Recorded Declaration

The following words were spoken aloud above the Grand Plaza of Aurelia on the forty-third day of the Month of Embers, in the year 1,203 of the Third Reckoning at the moment the Vault's first stones broke the surface of the earth. They were recorded in full by no fewer than seven scribes of the Guild, two priests of Oghma, and one very surprised pastry merchant. Their accuracy is not in dispute.

⬧

Izkon the Golden

Ancient Gold Greatwyrm — Sovereign of the Sunken Hoard — He Who Waited

“People of Aurelia. Hear my words. Long ago, before your grandparents' grandparents drew first breath, a vast portion of my hoard was taken from me. Not by force. By cunning. Hidden in a place no dragon's eye could find, behind wards older than your oldest kings. For centuries I searched. For centuries I failed. I have burned libraries seeking answers. I have questioned sages who did not survive the questioning. I have flown over every corner of this world and found nothing. Until now. Beneath your city, beneath the very stones upon which you stand, rests an ancient vault. Concealed by powers that predate your civilisation. Hidden specifically from the eyes of my kind. I cannot enter it. The magic forbids me. My fire cannot melt its gates. My claws cannot breach its walls. Every inch of it was built to keep me out. But you can enter. You small, soft, mortal things. You can walk right in.
The Challenge
Therefore, I issue this challenge to the peoples of the world:
to adventurers, mercenaries, scholars, hunters, heroes, and fools.
To anyone who breathes and hungers and dares.
— Descend into the Vault. — Find what was stolen from me. — Find and slay the one who stole it. — Bring me proof of both.
In return, I shall grant from my own hoard:
Gold beyond what your banks can hold. Relics older than your nations. Knowledge your scholars would commit crimes to possess. Favours from a creature who has watched empires rise and fall, and who does not forget his debts.

Name what lies within my power. I will not refuse those who succeed.
What Awaits
Many will enter.
Many will fail.
Some will find glory.
Most will be forgotten.

The Worthy may Descend.

⬧

Guild Addendum — Current Status of the Vault

Since the declaration above, Aurelia has become the greatest adventuring hub in the known world. Guild chapters from every major nation have established permanent presence within the city. Expeditions descend daily. The inns are full. The healers are busy. The coffin-makers are busier.

Four months of exploration have passed. No expedition has returned from the Vault's deepest reaches. Several have not returned at all.

What awaits below remains unknown.
